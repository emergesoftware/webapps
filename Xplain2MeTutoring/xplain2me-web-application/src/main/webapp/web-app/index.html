Our Tutors

Xplain2Me tutors are selectively sourced out from different universities throughout the country. Our tutors are either graduates or university students on their course to graduating. All of our tutors go through a screening process by qualification competence review and interviews.

Our Services

Our tutors travel to you and offer tutorials at the comfort of your own homes and at your request, we replace our tutors at no additional cost. The progress of students is monitored and corrective steps and timely adjustments are made where necessary to ensure set performance targets are met.

Our Rates

Our rates are affordable yet we offer the best service with utilization of qualified and trained tutors.We ensure our clients get value for their money by following up regularly on student performance and ensuring progress is being made.


A LITTLE ABOUT US

Xplain2Me was founded by well versed, resourceful and committed graduates who commenced private tutoring in and around the province, effectively and efficiently from 2009 till to date.
Ultimately imparting expertise, skills and knowledge has proved to be a successful journey in empowering students from all sorts of communities at large. In essence Xplain2Me has successfully and primarily endorsed its motto "simplify your learning", which has subsequently been proved through our clients results.

Customer Service

Our goal is to develop an understanding that our company is easy to do business with and to improve response time to client complaints and objectives. To help meet these goals, we will increase our client service staff, and implement a policy where our clients are guaranteed to receive a return phone call at the end of every week of operation. At Xplain2Me, we pride ourselves in the provision of excellent services through the implementation of our values which are accountability, punctuality, Integrity, transparency, reliability, diligence, consistency in ensuring good working relations and interactions with our clients.


OUR VISION AND MISSION

Our Mission

To operate the best specialty in the educational sector, in provision of quality services. We will optimize meeting our customers desired and ideal expectations through:

  • Emphasizing training and development of our tutors so as to continuously enhance our service delivery.
  • Continuously equipping our tutors with the required assistive techniques to appropriately propel the students in the right direction.
  • Continuously and effectively having an upward communication between management and clients.
  • Sufficient relationship focus by growing and enhancing relations with our existing and potential client.
  • Adequate service recovery by listening and advocating our client complaints through appropriate recovery mechanisms to alleviate service failures.
  • Emphasizing the training and development of tutors so as to enhance our service design and variability.
  • leading our industries in the application of appropriate technologies.
  • Delivering value to our customers, positive operating results and leading study techniques

Our Vision

To be South Africa's leader in educating, empowering and facilitating easy learning. We ensure and implement good standards of conduct to ensure that business does not impact negatively on interest of clients, and to subsequently succeed, in our services, continuously reaching our students, communities and ideal expectations at all times.
